Weekly B2B Auto Matching

tradeKorea recommends new business partners free of charge every week.

Summary
  • Member Information, It analyzes member information, activity information and rating information, and supports buyer and seller trade activities through tradeKorea's unique matching algorithm.
Provided service
  • For Buyers

    1.Automatic matching of sellers on tradeKorea’s list

    2.Additional recommendations from sellers selected by experts by item every month.

  • For Sellers

    1.Automatic matching of buyers on tradeKorea’s list

    2.Additional recommendation for buyers on KITA (Korea International Trade Association) list

Service Process

New matching information provided every week

  • New matching information is provided as cards every Monday at 00:00
  • Click the card to check the matching information; the buyer can send an inquiry about the product, and the seller can send a letter of intent to sell
How to Use

More information provided as the grade is upgraded

  • When the membership grade is upgraded, more matching information will be provided and there will be more opportunities for recommendation
  • Personal information registration supplement, tradeKorea participation (number of logins, matching card confirmation, etc.), the grade will be adjusted every Monday at 00:00
